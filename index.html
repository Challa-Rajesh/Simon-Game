Rules To Play

  • Start the game by pressing any key(Say Enter/return), from the keyboard.
  • The system randomly choosea a colour out of the four available colours.
  • your objective is to remember the sequence of the colours choosen by the system and to click on these colours in the same sequence as done by the system.
  • Your objective is to press the button correctly, matching the one played by the computer.
  • When the level increases, the system adds one colour to the sequence.
  • But, as a player you need to remember the whole sequence and to procede to next level you have to click the colours so as to match with the sequence that is choosen by the system.
  • If you click on the wrong colour which doesnot match the one with the sequence generated by the system, the GAME ENDS.
